Item 4

As an UTMOST user you can highlight and annotate text. When instructors highlight, they can copy the highlighted text for another purpose (e.g., send an e-mail to students). When students highlight and annotate, the system collects meta-data (the section that is annotated and the number of annotations). The text of the annotations is private to the students. Any highlighting that is done will be visible any time that the textbook is opened.
